Don't stress about stress!

Stress is something we all deal with, some of us cope better than others while others need support, this website will help you discover ways to manage stress effectively. Remember you are not alone, we are all in this together!

Helpful Tips to Manage Stress

Exercise Regularly

Physical activity can help reduce stress and improve mood. Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise most days and vary the type of activity.

Practice Mindfulness

Mindfulness techniques, such as meditation, can help reduce stress. Spend a few minutes each day focusing on your breath.

Get Enough Sleep

A good night's sleep is essential for managing stress and concentration levels. Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night.

Stay Connected

Spend time with friends and family. Social support is crucial for managing stress and improving mental health.

Limit Caffeine and Sugar

High caffeine and sugar intake can increase anxiety. Opt for herbal teas and whole foods to stabilize energy levels.

Establish a Routine

Having a daily routine can provide structure and predictability, helping to reduce stress and anxiety.

Engage in Hobbies

Take time to enjoy activities you love, whether it's reading, painting, or gardening. Hobbies can be a great stress reliever and also count as exercise

Practice Deep Breathing

Deep breathing exercises can help calm your mind. Try inhaling deeply for a count of four, holding for four, and exhaling for four.

Stay Hydrated

Drinking enough water is essential for overall health. Aim to drink at least 8 cups of water a day to help manage stress levels.

Stress Relief Affirmations

Whenever feeling under stress, read these affirmations to yourself
  • "I am in control of my thoughts, and I choose peace."
  • "I release all tension from my body and mind."
  • "Every deep breath I take helps me feel more calm."
  • "I am strong, resilient, and able to handle life's challenges."
  • "I focus on what I can control and let go of what I can't."
  • "With each breath, I feel my stress melting away."
